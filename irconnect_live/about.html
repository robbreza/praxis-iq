About the founder

He built IRconnect from the sell-side desk out.

Robert Breza created IRconnect after a career spanning technology development, corporate finance, Wall Street research, and capital markets advisory — encoding the discipline he learned across 20+ IPOs into software any IR team can run.

Before becoming a Managing Director at RBC Capital Markets, Rob spent nearly a decade in operating roles across technology, FP&A, corporate development, and M&A. Early on, he built a proprietary vulnerability-assessment platform for the AS/400 ecosystem — so building real software to solve a real problem is not a pivot for him. It’s the pattern.
