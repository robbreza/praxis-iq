What IRconnect does

Three of the hardest jobs in IR, run on real math.

Most IR tools give you a database and leave the judgment to you. IRconnect computes the judgment call — then shows every number behind it.

Investor Targeting

A transparent six-component score ranks every institution out of 100 using real 13F ownership data — not a purchased list.

  • Every component score visible — never a black box
  • Re-weights from your logged meeting outcomes

Guidance & Outlook Engine

Reads YTD revenue against a seasonally-weighted pace and recommends raise, reiterate, or narrow — with plain-English reasoning.

  • Every forward-looking claim flagged for legal review
  • Management always makes the final call

IR Risk Dashboard

A 24-indicator scorecard across six categories, computed from real sources wherever a real source exists.

  • Marked “not tracked” where it doesn’t — never guessed
  • Live investor objections move the board automatically

Why IRconnect is different

Computed, not guessed.

Every number in IRconnect traces to a real, disclosed source — market data, SEC filings, your logged investor conversations. Where no real data exists yet, the indicator says Not tracked instead of a guess dressed up as a metric. The green numbers are trustworthy precisely because the gray ones are honest.
