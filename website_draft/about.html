About Rob

Senior-level capital markets experience.

Robert Breza founded Praxis Point Advisory after a career spanning technology development, corporate finance, Wall Street research, and capital markets advisory.

Prior to becoming a Managing Director at RBC Capital Markets, Robert spent nearly a decade in corporate operating roles focused on technology, FP&A, corporate development, and M&A. Early in his career, he developed a proprietary vulnerability assessment platform for the AS/400 ecosystem and led enterprise technology initiatives supporting hundreds of mission-critical systems.

During more than two decades on Wall Street, Robert advised institutional investors and management teams across enterprise software, cybersecurity, cloud infrastructure, and emerging technology sectors. His experience includes more than 20 IPOs and follow-on offerings, extensive CEO and CFO engagement, and appearances on CNBC and Bloomberg Television.

Today, Praxis Point Advisory helps technology companies strengthen investor confidence, refine market positioning, and prepare for major public market milestones including IPOs, direct listings, follow-on offerings, and strategic transitions.
