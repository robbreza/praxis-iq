Earnings Cycle

Guidance & Outlook Decision Engine

The highest-stakes paragraph on any earnings call, drafted from real math instead of gut feel. The engine reads YTD revenue against a seasonally-weighted pace, computes what H2 needs to hit guidance, and recommends an action.

  • Recommends raise, reiterate, or narrow — with plain-English reasoning, not just a badge
  • Every forward-looking claim auto-tagged for legal review before it reaches the script
  • Management always makes the final call — the engine recommends, it doesn't decide

Risk Monitoring

IR Risk Dashboard

A 24-indicator scorecard across six risk categories — consensus dispersion, narrative risk, ownership shifts, disclosure compliance, and more — computed from real data wherever a real source exists.

  • No fabricated benchmarks: where no real source exists yet, the indicator says "not tracked" — not a guess dressed up as a metric
  • A real logged investor objection or narrative gap moves the dashboard automatically
  • Every colored indicator is trustworthy precisely because the gray ones are honest

Analyst & Buy-Side Mail

AI-Classified Inbox

Every inbound analyst or investor email is automatically read and classified — an updated model, a research note, a meeting request, a conference invite — and routed to the right person, pre-filled with the fields that matter.

  • Research notes get a CFA-lens extraction: rating/PT change, valuation method, thesis vs. consensus, catalysts and risks
  • Marked Internal Use Only — built around internal circulation, never external redistribution of licensed research
  • Turns "reviewed / not reviewed" into a decision the Head of IR can make in seconds
